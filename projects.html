Social buzz

I completed a minor project on data analytics, where I analyzed datasets provided for the SocialBuzz platform to identify the top 5 most popular content categories. In this project, each type of user reaction (like, love, laugh, etc.) was assigned a unique score based on its significance. Using the provided datasets, I analyzed user reactions to various posts, quantifying engagement by applying the reaction scores. The analysis helped me determine the top 5 content categories that received the highest overall engagement, offering valuable insights into user preferences and content effectiveness on the platform.
